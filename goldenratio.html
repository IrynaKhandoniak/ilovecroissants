F-Pattern & Golden Ratio in Web Design

F-Pattern

Users scan pages in an "F" shape: top-left, vertical scan down the left, and a smaller horizontal scan.

Tip: Place important content at the top-left and along the left side for better engagement.

Golden Ratio

The Golden Ratio (1:1.618) ensures visually pleasing proportions for balanced design.

Tip: Use the ratio to divide sections and create a harmonious layout.
