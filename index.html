🥐 I love Croissants 🥐

A croissant is a golden, buttery marvel of French baking, with layers that flake and melt in perfect harmony. Its crisp exterior and soft, airy interior offer a simple yet indulgent pleasure, a reminder to savor life's small moments. People should love it for its delicate craftsmanship and the sensory delight it brings—both a treat for the taste buds and a celebration of the art of slowing down.
